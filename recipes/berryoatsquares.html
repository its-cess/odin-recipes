Berry Oat Squares

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup vegan margarine
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oat flakes
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup jam
  • 1 tsp flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Lightly oil an 8x8-inch baking pan. Set aside.
  3. In a small saucepan, melt the margarine. Set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, stir together the oats, flour, sugar, and melted margarine.
  5. Set aside 1 cup and press the remaining mixture into the baking pan.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes.
  7. In a medium bowl, stir together the blueberries, jam, and flour.
  8. Spread evenly over baked crust.
  9. Spread oat mixture evenly over top of berries.
  10. Bake for another 20 minutes.
  11. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
