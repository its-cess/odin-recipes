Black Bean Sweet Potato Burritos
Description:
This vegan dish is healthy and packed full of flavor!
Ingredients:
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup vegetable stock
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp ground mustard
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 cup vegan "cheese"
Steps:
  1. Boil the sweet potatoes.
  2. Saute onions in oil until translucent.
  3. Add the black beans, stock, garlic, chili powder, mustard, cumin, and salt.
    Simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Drain sweet potatoes, return to pot, and mash with a potato masher.
  5. When bean mixture is finished, mash with potato masher and set aside.
  6. Heat tortillas on tortilla warmer.
  7. Fill burritos with potatoes, bean mixture, salsa, and cheese.
  8. Enjoy!
